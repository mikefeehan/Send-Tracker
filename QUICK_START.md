# 🍹 Quick Start — Maui Send Tracker

## First Time? Follow These Steps:

### 1️⃣ Open the App
- **Link:** https://maui-send-tracker.web.app
- Works on any phone, tablet, or computer

### 2️⃣ Set Up Your Profile
- Enter your name
- (Optional) Upload a photo of yourself
- Tap **Let's Go 🎉**

### 3️⃣ Add to Home Screen (Recommended)
When asked: **"Add to Home Screen"**
- Makes the app much faster
- Works just like a regular app icon
- Takes 2 seconds!

### 4️⃣ Log Your First Drink 🍺
1. Tap the **+** button (bottom right)
2. Choose drink type: Beer 🍺 | Wine 🍷 | Shot 🥃 | Cocktail 🍸
3. Tap **📍** to detect your location (Maui spots only)
4. Upload a photo (proof or it didn't happen! 📸)
5. Add a fun description (optional)
6. Tap **Send It!**

---

## 📊 How It Works

**Points System:**
- Beer = 1 point
- Cocktail, Wine, Shot = 2 points each

**Leaderboard:**
- **#1** wins the day 👑
- Rankings update in real-time
- See today's and past days

**Badges:**
Unlock achievements as you drink:
- First blood 🩸
- Hat trick 🎩
- Golden hour 🌇
- And more!
